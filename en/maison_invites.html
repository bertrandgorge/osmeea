A centuries-old farmhouse The Mas

The mas d'OSMEEA comprises 4 units, connected to a common living area including a dining area, an equipped kitchen and a living room. This living space has a large bay window linking to outdoor spaces.

Off-season (from September 1st to June 30th), it is possible to rent by the night and by the room (the common area is then shared).
In season (from July 1st to August 31st), the mas is rented weekly and in its entirety (see rates).

The maximum capacity of the mas is 12 people

All suites are equipped with:

  • WIFI (fibered)
  • a shower room with WC
  • a courtesy tray
  • a hair dryer
  • towels
  • bed linen
  • a reversible air conditioning

The organization of the building allows to use each suite independently of each other.

Suites:

  • Aloe Room
  • Mimosa Suite
  • Bougainvillea Suite
  • Jasmine Room

ALOE Room

On the 1st floor of the mas, a sober and comfortable ​​16m² bedroom, with quality bedding (140x200 cm).

MIMOSA Suite

25m² master suite with a sleeping area and a lounge area (including a 140cm sofa bed).

The bedding is composed of a twin bed (2 x 80x200 cm).

BOUGAINVILLIEA Suite

45m² master suite with sitting area (including a 160cm sofa bed), a desk and a fitted kitchen (with sink, microwave, kettle and fridge).

The bedding is composed of a twin bed (2 x 80x200cm).

Separated by a staircase, the Mimosa and Bougainvillea suites can be connected, for large families.
