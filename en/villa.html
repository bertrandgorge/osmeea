A Century Old Bastide The Villa

With its five bedrooms and generous dimensions, this country house can comfortably accommodate up to 9 people.

6 bedroom villa of 280m²:

Ground floor: Living room with lounge, dining room, exit to garden. Fully equipped kitchen. Guest WC.
First floor: Living room TV with Internet. Master double bedroom (160cm), Bathroom / Shower. Separate WC. Laundry with washing machine and dryer.
2nd floor: 1 single bedroom (90cm). 1 double bedroom (140cm). Shower room / WC. 2 double bedrooms (160cm) including 1 with shower room.

In total 5 bedrooms (4 doubles and 1 single)
